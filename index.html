A calm afternoon for families

A welcoming café meetup for parents, carers, and coffee lovers.

Bring kids under 8 for a safe afternoon daycare session while adults relax, connect, and enjoy great coffee with less stress.

Book a spot Plan your visit

How it works

1) Check in together

Families arrive between 1:30–2:00 PM and meet the childcare team.

2) Kids enjoy play-based care

Children join age-appropriate activities, stories, and quiet nap corners.

3) Adults recharge

Parents and other adults can work, chat, or simply enjoy a peaceful coffee break.

Why families and communities love it

  • ✅ Friendly staff trained in early childhood support
  • ✅ Secure sign-in / sign-out for every child
  • ✅ Quiet zone, social tables, and Wi-Fi for adults
  • ✅ Inclusive for single parents, couples, carers, and friends
  • ✅ Affordable sessions with sibling discounts
